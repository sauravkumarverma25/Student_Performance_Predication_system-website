About Me

Welcome to our innovative Student Performance System System! The Student Performance System is designed to efficiently manage and analyze student performance data for both teachers and students.The system aims to provide valuable insights into academic achievements, helping educators make data-driven decisions and aiding students in tracking their progress.

  • Teacher Dashboard:
  • - Content Upload: Teachers can upload study materials, including notes, links, and recommendations, tailored to students' performance levels (Low, Medium, High).
  • - CSV Upload: Teachers can upload CSV files containing student performance data for analysis.

  • Student Dashboard:
  • - Performance Classification: The system classifies students into performance levels (Low, Medium, High) based on their total scores.
  • - Material Recommendations: Students receive personalized study materials recommended by teachers according to their performance levels.
